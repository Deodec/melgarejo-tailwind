Emperor Jarvan IV Ruler of the Demaxian Empire Age: 32, Leadership: Unmatched In the vast expanse of the cosmos, the Demaxian Empire stands as a shining beacon of order and authority, built on principles of honor, strength, and an unwavering belief in the empire's supremacy. At the heart of this galactic dominion is Emperor Jarvan IV, a proud and unyielding leader, who has sworn to bring unity and peace to a chaotic universe—by force if necessary. Jarvan IV was raised in the corridors of Demaxian power, groomed from birth to be the ideal ruler. His upbringing was strict, filled with the discipline of the Empire’s military traditions and the weight of his forefathers' legacy.
